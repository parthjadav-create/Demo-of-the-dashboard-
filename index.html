Out of Station Visit — Counting Rules
An Out of Station Visit is counted when a trainer travels to a city different from their base city.
The round-trip distance must be more than 100 km to qualify.
Each Out of Station visit with status "Done" is credited as 2 visits toward the trainer's daily done count.
The Target in this view is set at 3.0 visits per working day (adjustable in Settings).
The Out of Station Done column shows raw count completed. The Effective Done column reflects the count after the 2× credit is applied.
